Hi! I'm Daniele, a computer engineer and musician (so I like to believe) born in Naples, raised in Florence and currently living in Milan. I have 10 years of experience in software development, with a focus on backend engineering, software architecture design and database optimization. In my career I have designed, developed or coordinated more than 50 projects, from the simplest showcase sites to complex commercial and management systems for large companies.

Performance, best practices, high quality code and attention to detail are hallmarks of my work philosophy. A curious tinkerer at heart with a strong background in Linux systems, I am constantly dedicated to staying up-to-date on the latest technologies and trends in the industry in order to be able to offer innovative, robust, scalable and secure software solutions that exceed customer expectations.

In the last several years I had the opportunity to manage, coordinate and motivate many work groups, which allowed me to refine my teamwork and leadership skills, not only at work but also in associations.
I firmly believe that each of us can make a difference in the world and should do our best to help improve society, which is why I have been an active member of Rotaract since 2018. Among the many roles held, I've served as the Head of IT for District 2071 (Tuscany) and I have been honored as a Paul Harris Fellow, Rotary International's highest acknowledgement, thanks to the technological initiatives undertaken particularly during the pandemic period.
